Parameters and Specifications

VoltGlass® is designed to cover a wide range of applications – from standard interior heating to special exterior use.

Basic Specifications

ParameterValue
Supply voltage100–250 V AC, 50–60 Hz
Maximum surface temperature55–60 °C (adjustable)
Power output (standard)25–90 W/m²
Power output (special applications)up to 120 W/m²
Warm-up time to full powermax. 30 minutes
Technology lifespanup to 25 years

Power and Coverage

Standard Heating (25–90 W/m²)

Design depends on system function and building properties. Key factors:

  • Building type (older building vs. passive house)
  • Heat losses of the building
  • Required function (primary/supplementary heat source)
  • Glazing area in the room

High Power (up to 120 W/m²)

For special applications, power can be increased up to 120 W/m². This mode is intended for:

  • Snow and ice melting – roof glazing, skylights, canopies
  • Fog prevention – already at 25 W/m² (shop windows, display cases, glass facades)
  • Extreme climate conditions – mountain areas, northern regions

Perceived Heat

Feel the heat within seconds. Full power in max. 30 minutes.

Power Supply and Compatibility

Power Supply

VoltGlass® is ready for standard electrical grids 50–60 Hz, without transformer:

  • Compatibility – with various electrical grids
  • Direct power – connection to existing grid
  • Stable operation – even during voltage drops

Regulation

Thermostat regulation, like underfloor heating:

  • Set temperature in room or on glass surface
  • Manual or remote control (smart home)
  • After reaching temperature, switches on only when it drops by 1–2 °C

Dimensions and Configuration

VoltGlass® is manufactured to order according to the specific project:

  • Minimum size: 300 × 300 mm
  • Maximum size: limited only by handling (typically up to 3 × 4 m)
  • Thickness: double glazing from 17 mm, triple glazing from 24 mm; heating glass itself from 4 mm
  • Shape: geometric shape according to client requirements
