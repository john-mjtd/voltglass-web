Parameters and Specifications

VoltGlass® is designed to cover a wide range of applications – from standard interior heating to special exterior use.

Basic Specifications

ParameterValue
Supply voltage100–250 V AC
Maximum surface temperature50 °C
Power output (standard)25–90 W/m²
Power output (special applications)up to 120 W/m²
Warm-up time to full powerapprox. 30 minutes
Lifespansame as insulating glass (25+ years)

Power and Coverage

Standard Heating (25–90 W/m²)

At standard power output, 1 m² of VoltGlass® heated glass can effectively heat 8–12 m² of floor area. The exact value depends on:

  • Building thermal insulation
  • Climate zone
  • Required indoor temperature
  • Glazing orientation

High Power (up to 120 W/m²)

For special applications, power can be increased up to 120 W/m². This mode is intended for:

  • Snow and ice melting – roof glazing, skylights, canopies
  • Fog prevention – shop windows, display cases, glass facades
  • Extreme climate conditions – mountain areas, northern regions

Perceived Heat

VoltGlass® starts heating immediately after switching on. Although reaching maximum surface temperature takes about 30 minutes, the feeling of warmth is noticeable within a few minutes.

Power Supply and Compatibility

Wide Voltage Range

VoltGlass® operates in the 100–250 V range, ensuring:

  • Universal use – compatibility with electrical grids worldwide
  • Photovoltaic ready – direct power from solar panels
  • Stable operation – tolerance to grid voltage fluctuations

Self-regulation

The VoltGlass® heating layer has built-in self-regulating capability:

  • Power automatically adjusts to current temperature
  • No risk of overheating even during long-term operation
  • No complex control electronics required

Dimensions and Configuration

VoltGlass® is manufactured to order according to the specific project:

  • Minimum size: 300 × 300 mm
  • Maximum size: limited only by handling (typically up to 3 × 6 m)
  • Thickness: same as standard insulating glass (24–48 mm)
  • Shape: rectangle, square, special shapes on request
