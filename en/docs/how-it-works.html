How VoltGlass® Works

VoltGlass® is a patented Czech technology that transforms ordinary insulating glass into an intelligent heating element. Unlike traditional heating systems that heat the air in a room, VoltGlass® uses the principle of radiant heat – the same way the sun warms us.

Technology Principle

The basis is tempered low-emissivity glass with an invisible conductive layer – 50–60× thinner than a human hair. The patented manufacturing process ensures even heating across the entire surface.

When connected to 230 V mains, the layer converts electricity into infrared radiant heat. This heats people and surfaces directly – the air is heated subsequently.

Key Principle

VoltGlass® transforms the window into an active heating surface. The glass transfers heat through radiation while simultaneously creating a thermal barrier on its surface that prevents heat from escaping through the window. As a result, it doesn't cool the interior but helps keep the heat inside.

VoltGlass® Radiant Heat

Infrared radiation does not primarily heat the air, but transfers energy directly to surfaces and people. The result is:

  • Higher thermal comfort
  • Elimination of the cold glass effect
  • You feel the heat within seconds of switching on
  • Heating of objects and heat returned back to the room

What Makes VoltGlass® Unique

Transparency

The heating layer is 50–60× thinner than a human hair. The glass looks like ordinary insulating glass – no visible elements, no wires, no changes in optical properties. The heating system is completely transparent.

Building Integration

Installed like standard insulating glass – in new or existing windows. 230 V power without transformers. No visible heating elements, clean interior.

Elimination of Cold Surfaces

Large glazed areas often mean a feeling of cold. VoltGlass® eliminates this – the window becomes a source of heat.

Heat Direction

Low-emissivity coating directs heat inward. Minimum escapes, maximum flows inside. The glass functions as both heating and insulating element.

Czech Patent

Protected by Czech patent No. 309675. Unique solution developed in the Czech Republic with global potential.
