How VoltGlass® Works

VoltGlass® is a patented Czech technology that transforms ordinary architectural glass into an intelligent heating element. Unlike traditional heating systems that heat the air in a room, VoltGlass® uses the principle of radiant heat – the same way the sun warms us.

Technology Principle

Inside the glass is an integrated special transparent microstructure – a thin conductive layer that is invisible to the naked eye. This layer is applied with micrometric precision using advanced manufacturing processes.

When connected to electrical power (standard 230 V), the conductive layer begins to generate heat. This heat is in the form of infrared radiation directed into the room, where it directly heats surfaces and people – not the air.

Key Principle

VoltGlass® doesn't create heat in the room that would then escape through windows. Instead, it generates heat directly in the glazing – exactly where the greatest heat losses occur with ordinary windows.

Radiant Heat vs. Convection Heating

Traditional Convection Systems

Radiators and underfloor heating warm the air in the room. Warm air rises to the ceiling, cold air sinks to the floor. This creates circulation that:

  • Stirs up dust and allergens
  • Creates uneven temperature distribution (warm at ceiling, cold at floor)
  • Causes a feeling of "dry air"
  • Requires higher air temperature to achieve thermal comfort

VoltGlass® Radiant Heat

Infrared radiation from heated glass passes through the air without heating it and transfers energy directly to surfaces and people. The result is:

  • Even heat distribution throughout the space
  • No air circulation – ideal for allergy sufferers
  • Feeling of warmth even at lower air temperature (energy savings)
  • Natural, pleasant sensation similar to sunlight

What Makes VoltGlass® Unique

Invisibility

The heating layer is completely transparent. The glass looks like ordinary architectural glass – no visible elements, no wires, no changes in optical properties. The heating system is literally invisible.

Building Integration

VoltGlass® is installed like standard insulating glass. Electrodes are integrated into the window frame, connection is simple and safe. No technical room needed, no piping, no radiators.

Elimination of Cold Surfaces

The biggest weakness of modern buildings with large glazed areas is the feeling of cold from windows. VoltGlass® completely eliminates this problem – the window becomes a source of heat instead of a source of cold.

Heat Direction

Thanks to a special low-emissivity coating, heat is directed into the room. Minimum energy escapes outside, maximum heat flows inside. The glass thus functions as both a heating and insulating element simultaneously.

Czech Patent

VoltGlass® technology is protected by Czech patent No. 309675. It is a unique solution developed in the Czech Republic with potential for global application.
