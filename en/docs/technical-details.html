Manufacturing Technical Details

VoltGlass® is created by combining several advanced manufacturing technologies. Each pane goes through a strict production process that ensures consistent quality, safety and long lifespan.

Manufacturing Process

1. Glass Preparation

The base is high-quality float glass that undergoes thorough inspection and cleaning.

2. Conductive Layer Application

A special conductive layer is applied to the prepared glass with micrometric precision. This layer is transparent, uniform and durable.

3. Laser Structuring

The conductive layer is laser-structured to achieve the required electrical resistance and power distribution.

4. Tempering

Glass with the conductive layer undergoes thermal tempering, which ensures mechanical strength 4–5× higher than ordinary glass.

5. Electrode Integration

Electrodes for power connection are integrated into the glass edges and are completely hidden in the window frame.

6. Insulating Glass Assembly

The heated glass is combined with other panes into an insulating unit with a gap filled with argon or krypton.

Made in Czech Republic

VoltGlass® will be manufactured in the Czech Republic using state-of-the-art technologies.

Safety

Electrical Safety

  • Conductive layer insulation – layer is enclosed inside the glass
  • Dielectric spacer elements – electrically non-conductive components
  • Moisture protection – hermetic edge sealing

Fire Safety

  • Maximum surface temperature 50 °C – far below ignition point
  • Self-regulation prevents overheating
  • Glass is a non-combustible A1 class material

Mechanical Safety

  • Impact resistance due to tempering
  • When broken, it crumbles into small blunt pieces
  • Option to combine with lamination for higher safety

Certification

VoltGlass® will be certified according to applicable European standards for construction products (CE), electrical safety and electromagnetic compatibility.

Quality Control

Input Control

Quality control of input glass, verification of conductive material and component parameters.

In-process Control

Measurement of conductive layer thickness and uniformity, laser structuring control, electrical parameter verification.

Output Control

100% electrical test of each pane, temperature distribution measurement with thermal camera, visual inspection of optical quality.

Lifespan and Warranty

  • Expected lifespan: 25+ years
  • No performance degradation – conductive layer doesn't age
  • No maintenance – maintenance-free operation
