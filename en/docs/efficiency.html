Why VoltGlass® Is Efficient

The energy efficiency of VoltGlass® stems from a fundamentally different approach to heating. While traditional systems fight heat losses, VoltGlass® addresses them directly at the source.

Energy Savings Principle

The Problem with Traditional Systems

Classic heating systems work on the principle of heating air in the room:

  • Heat rises to the ceiling where it's not needed
  • Windows and glazed surfaces remain cold
  • A feeling of cold near windows even at high air temperature
  • System must compensate for losses through glazing with higher output

The VoltGlass® Solution

VoltGlass® generates heat directly in the glazing – exactly where the greatest heat losses occur:

  • Elimination of cold surfaces – window becomes a heat source
  • Reduced heat losses – warm glass doesn't let as much energy escape
  • Lower air temperature – radiant heat creates comfort even at lower temperature
  • Instant response – glass heats only when needed

Savings in Practice

Thanks to the radiant heat principle, the same thermal comfort can be achieved at an air temperature 2–3 °C lower. Each degree less means approximately 6% energy savings.

Low-Emissivity Coating

Heat Reflection Inward

The low-emissivity coating reflects infrared radiation back into the room. Heat generated by the heating layer stays in the interior. Reflection efficiency reaches up to 95%.

Insulating Properties

The same coating also prevents heat escape from the room even when the glass isn't heating. VoltGlass® thus functions as highly efficient insulating glass.

Regulation

Regulation via thermostat, similar to underfloor heating:

  • Flexible settings – temperature can be set in the room or on the glass surface
  • Smart home – quick temperature changes manually or remotely
  • Efficient operation – after reaching temperature, glass switches on only when it drops by 1–2 °C
  • Simple installation – standard thermostat, no complex electronics

Operating Costs

Zero Service Costs

No moving parts, no inspections, no maintenance. Lifespan up to 25 years.

Low Investment Costs

When using VoltGlass® as the primary heating system, costs for boiler room, piping, radiators and chimney are eliminated.

Compatibility with Renewable Sources

100–250 V range enables direct power from photovoltaics. With battery storage, energy self-sufficiency is achievable.
