Documentation / Applications

Where VoltGlass® Is Used

VoltGlass® is used wherever glass should be more than just an infill – an active part of comfort and building function. It turns glazed surfaces into elements that increase thermal comfort, reduce condensation, prevent icing, and preserve clean architecture without obtrusive heating elements.

Windows, Balconies and Conservatories

Windows, Balconies and Loggias

Large glazed areas are often where interiors lose comfort. VoltGlass® transforms a window from a passive surface into a functional element – as a primary or secondary heat source, it reduces the cold glass effect and helps minimize heat losses.

Conservatories

VoltGlass® helps shift the dew point, reduce fogging and condensation on glass, and preserve maximum natural light. The conservatory becomes a more usable space for a larger part of the year.

Ideal for Passive Houses

In passive and low-energy houses, the low heat demand allows heating to be covered solely by glazed surfaces – no radiators or piping needed.

Roofs, Skylights and Canopies

Roof and Mansard Windows

VoltGlass® effectively addresses snow and ice on glass, increases light transmission, and contributes to safer roof structure operation. Suitable for new projects and replacements in existing windows.

Skylights and Glass Roofs

For hard-to-reach glazed surfaces, it reduces icing, snow load, and light loss. Maintains light transmission even in harsh conditions and reduces maintenance needs.

Canopies and Shelters

The heated surface accelerates snow and ice melting, reduces condensation, and preserves the representative appearance of the building. Function and aesthetics in one.

Pools, Saunas and Wet Areas

In high-humidity spaces, VoltGlass® reduces window fogging and maintains visual and user comfort. Suitable for pools, saunas, and other areas where a clear view is needed even in demanding conditions.

Commercial and Public Buildings

Office buildings, shop windows, glass walls, halls, heritage buildings, and public spaces – VoltGlass® enables better use of large glazed areas, increased thermal comfort, and clean architecture without obtrusive elements.

Custom Solutions, New Builds and Renovations

Custom and Design Solutions

The technology is not limited to standard formats – it is designed for various shapes, sizes, and atypical structures where conventional solutions fall short.

New Builds

In new projects, it expands the possibilities of working with glazing. It can completely replace a traditional heating system – no boiler room, piping, or radiators needed.

Renovations

For modernizations, the advantage is minimal intervention in the building – VoltGlass® can be used where the investor seeks the cleanest solution without complicated construction work.
