Documentation / Applications

Where VoltGlass® Is Used

VoltGlass® is a universal solution for a wide range of projects – from family homes through commercial buildings to special industrial applications.

Residential Projects

Family Homes

In family homes, VoltGlass® brings a revolution in heating concepts. Instead of radiators, simply install heated glass windows.

  • Living rooms – large glazing without feeling cold
  • Bedrooms – quiet heating without air circulation
  • Bathrooms – pleasant warmth without radiators
  • Conservatories – year-round use without additional heating

Ideal for Passive Houses

VoltGlass® is an ideal solution for passive and low-energy houses, where low heat demand allows heating to be covered solely by glazed surfaces.

Commercial and Public Buildings

Offices

Modern office buildings with large glazed facades suffer from cold surface problems. VoltGlass® enables:

  • Flexible workspace arrangement
  • Even thermal comfort throughout the open-space
  • Reduced heating operating costs

Hotels and Wellness

  • Rooms with views – large windows without cold
  • Wellness zones – glazed saunas, relaxation rooms
  • Lobby and reception – representative glazed spaces

New Builds vs. Renovations

New Builds

In new builds, VoltGlass® can completely replace traditional heating systems:

  • Investment cost savings – no boiler room, piping, radiators needed
  • More usable space – no technical room
  • Architectural freedom – no restrictions due to heating elements

Renovations

For renovations, VoltGlass® is ideal due to minimal construction intervention:

  • Glass replacement – just replace glass in existing frame
  • No demolition – no intervention in floors and walls
  • Quick implementation – modernization within days

Exterior Applications

Roof Glazing and Skylights

VoltGlass® in high-power mode (up to 120 W/m²) ensures:

  • Automatic snow and ice melting
  • Prevention of structural damage from snow weight
  • Year-round light transmission

Glass Facades

  • Prevention of shop window fogging in winter months
  • Thermal comfort for customers and staff
  • Space savings – no radiators in sales area
