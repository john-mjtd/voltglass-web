We’re Launching a Bond Sale: The Next Key Step After the Patent

Securing our international patent for VoltGlass® heated glass was a major milestone. Now comes the next essential step on the road to market – financing a manufacturing plant through a bond sale.

Why bonds and why now

The patent protects our know-how and gives us room for long-term growth. But to move heated glass from pilot stage into real-world projects, we need our own manufacturing capacity. Bonds allow us to take this step quickly and with focus – build and commission a manufacturing plant.

A manufacturing plant in the Czech Republic

We’re preparing production in the Czech Republic – where the technology was born and where we have the team, labs, and supply chain to make it happen. The goal is to build a manufacturing base that will supply the Czech market while preparing for international expansion.

The sale is already active

The bond sale is already underway—for example via our partner Finte: https://finte.cz/inzerce/otevreni-a-zprovozneni-vyrobniho-zavodu.

Additional patents in Europe and the US

Alongside the manufacturing rollout, we’re pursuing patents in additional European countries and the United States. At the same time, we’re developing a licensing model that will make it possible to produce and sell heated glass in markets we can’t reach directly.

Buildings without radiators. Energy where it makes sense.

Our vision is a world where heated glass replaces traditional heating – buildings with clean design, greater comfort, and lower energy consumption. VoltGlass® is making that future happen. And right now is when we decide how fast it becomes reality.
