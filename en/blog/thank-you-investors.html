Thank You to Our Investors for Believing in VoltGlass

Your trust in our heated glass project and your decision to invest in our bonds means more to us than words can express. Thanks to you, VoltGlass is moving into the implementation phase.

The belief that drives our vision

When we presented our vision – to bring innovative heated glass to market that will change how we heat our homes and commercial spaces – we knew it was an ambitious plan. We also knew that without the support of people who believe in this project, it would remain just an idea on paper.

You are those people. Your investment is not just a financial matter – it's a vote of confidence in our technology, our team, and the future we're building together.

Where we stand today

Thanks to your contributions, the VoltGlass project is moving into the implementation phase faster than we originally dared to hope:

  • We have a production facility secured
  • Technology suppliers are agreed and prepared
  • We have pre-orders for nearly 50% of our planned annual production capacity

Our goal is to start production and sales in the second half of this year. Every day, we work to keep this timeline on track.

Our commitment to you

The entire VoltGlass team is fully aware of the responsibility we carry. Your investment is not just capital to us – it's proof that you believe in us. And we will do everything in our power not to disappoint that trust.

We promise you:

  • Maximum transparency at every stage of the project
  • Careful stewardship of every invested crown
  • Tireless work to make VoltGlass succeed in the market
  • Regular updates on project progress

Invitation to an open day

Once we have our production facility fully operational, we would love to show all of you where your investment has gone. We will organize an open day specifically for our investors, where you can see the technology, tour the production line, and personally meet the people working on the project.

There's still room for new investors

If you're reading about our project for the first time and it caught your interest, we have good news – our bond offering is still ongoing. You too can be part of this story.

Investing in VoltGlass means investing in:

  • Innovative patented Czech technology with global potential
  • A more ecological way of heating
  • A project with a clear business strategy and existing demand
  • A team with experience, determination, and vision

Interested in investing?

Visit our For Investors section or contact us for more information.

Together toward success

In the coming months, you will regularly receive updates on how the project is progressing. We want you to be part of this journey – not just as investors, but as partners who helped VoltGlass move from vision to reality.

Once again – thank you from the bottom of our hearts. Your trust commits us to doing excellent work. And that's exactly what we'll do.

With respect and gratitude,
The VoltGlass Team
