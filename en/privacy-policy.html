PRIVACY POLICY

voltglass.cz

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data in connection with the operation of the voltglass.cz website.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is the operator of the voltglass.cz website, whose contact details can be found on this Website.

2. What Personal Data We Process

As part of operating the website, we process the following personal data:

2.1 Email Address (Newsletter)

If you subscribe to our newsletter through the form on the Website, we process your email address for the purpose of sending information, news and marketing communications.

2.2 Analytical Data (Google Analytics)

The Website uses Google Analytics, which collects anonymized data about website traffic, including IP address, browser type, visit duration and other statistical data. This data is used to improve the functionality and user experience of the Website.

3. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For sending newsletters, we process your email address based on your consent, which you can withdraw at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link.
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytical purposes, we use Google Analytics based on our legitimate interest in improving the functionality of the Website.

4. Data Retention Period

  • Email Address: We process until you withdraw your consent to receive the newsletter.
  • Analytical Data: Google Analytics retains data for 26 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

5. Sharing Personal Data with Third Parties

We may share your personal data with the following entities:

  • Google LLC: As part of using Google Analytics. Google processes data in accordance with its privacy policy available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Your Rights

In connection with the processing of personal data, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to obtain information about what personal data we process about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You have the right to request restriction of processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on consent, you have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.

7. Security of Personal Data

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction or alteration. Only authorized persons have access to personal data.

8. Cookies

The Website uses cookies in connection with Google Analytics. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser that help us analyze the use of the Website. You can manage your consent to the use of cookies in your browser settings.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. The current version is always available on this Website. We will inform you of significant changes via email or a notice on the Website.

10. Contact and Complaints

If you have any questions about the processing of your personal data or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us using the contact details provided on the Website.

You also have the right to file a complaint with the supervisory authority, which is the Office for Personal Data Protection (https://www.uoou.cz).
